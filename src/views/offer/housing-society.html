Society Frequently Asked Questions

How much can our society save with solar? expand_more
Most housing societies can reduce their common area electricity bills by 70-90% depending on their consumption patterns and available rooftop space. With SolarForce's solutions, societies typically see annual savings ranging from ₹2-10 lakhs, with the system paying for itself in 3-4 years.
Is there a government subsidy for housing societies? expand_more
Yes, housing societies are eligible for government subsidies under various state and central schemes. Subsidies typically range from ₹18,000 per kW, with maximum caps varying by state. SolarForce assists with the entire subsidy application process and documentation.
How do we apply for the subsidy? expand_more
SolarForce handles the entire subsidy application process on your behalf. We prepare all required documentation, liaise with government departments, and track the application until disbursement. Our team ensures compliance with all technical and administrative requirements.
Does our society need an AGM (Annual General Meeting) approval to install solar? expand_more
Yes, most housing societies require AGM approval for installing a solar system since it involves common area usage and society funds. SolarForce provides comprehensive presentation materials and financial projections to help your management committee present the proposal effectively to residents.
What is the typical payback period for a solar system? expand_more
The payback period typically ranges from 3-4 years depending on your electricity tariffs, consumption patterns, available subsidies, and choice of components. After the payback period, your society enjoys 20+ years of virtually free electricity for common areas.
What happens if solar generation is lower than expected? expand_more
SolarForce provides a performance guarantee. If your system generates less than the promised units due to equipment issues (excluding weather variations), we compensate you for the shortfall. Our advanced monitoring system tracks performance in real-time to ensure optimal generation.
How much rooftop space is needed for a solar installation? expand_more
Approximately 100 square feet of shadow-free rooftop space is needed for every 1 kW of solar capacity. Our engineers conduct a detailed site survey including shadow analysis to determine the optimal system size for your available rooftop area.
What are the different financing options available? expand_more
SolarForce offers flexible financing: CAPEX (society owns the system, maximum savings), OPEX/RESCO (zero investment, pay discounted tariff for generated power), and solar loans with attractive interest rates. Our team helps you choose the best model based on your society's financial situation.
How do we ensure the safety and longevity of the solar installation? expand_more
SolarForce uses premium components with 25+ year warranties and advanced mounting structures engineered to withstand extreme weather conditions. Our installations include lightning protection, proper earthing, and waterproofing to ensure a leak-free roof. We also offer comprehensive AMC packages for ongoing maintenance.
What maintenance does a solar system require? expand_more
Solar systems require minimal maintenance—primarily periodic cleaning of panels (every 15-30 days depending on location) and annual technical inspections. SolarForce offers Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) that include regular cleaning, system health checks, inverter monitoring, and prompt issue resolution.
Do we need roof access rights from top-floor residents? expand_more
Usually, the terrace is considered a common area belonging to the society. However, we recommend checking your specific society by-laws. SolarForce installs elevated structures that still allow the roof to be used for other purposes, minimizing any concerns from top-floor residents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does solar work on cloudy days?

expand_more

Yes, solar panels can still generate electricity on cloudy days, although their output is reduced. They utilize diffuse sunlight. However, during heavy rain or night, your home will draw power from the grid or your battery backup.

What maintenance is required?

expand_more

Solar systems are very low maintenance. We recommend cleaning the panels every 3-6 months to remove dust and debris for maximum efficiency. Our Maintenance Packages cover this for you.

Is financing available?

expand_more

Absolutely. We partner with leading banks to offer solar loans with attractive interest rates. The EMI is often lower than your current electricity bill savings.

How long does installation take?

expand_more

The physical installation on your roof typically takes 1 to 2 days for a standard residential system. However, the permitting and net-metering approval process with the local utility may take 2-4 weeks.

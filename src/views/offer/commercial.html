Commercial Solar FAQs

What is the typical ROI for commercial solar? expand_more
Commercial solar systems typically achieve ROI within 3-5 years, depending on your electricity consumption, tariff rates, and available roof space. After the payback period, you enjoy 20+ years of virtually free electricity, resulting in significant long-term savings.
What tax benefits are available for commercial solar? expand_more
Businesses can benefit from Accelerated Depreciation, allowing you to claim up to 40% depreciation in the first year. This significantly reduces taxable income and improves your year-one ROI. Additionally, solar projects often qualify for priority sector lending with lower interest rates.
How much roof space is needed for a commercial installation? expand_more
Approximately 100 square feet of shadow-free rooftop space is needed for every 1 kW of solar capacity. For example, a 50 kW system would require about 5,000 sq ft. Our engineers conduct a detailed site survey including shadow analysis to determine the optimal system size for your available space.
Will solar installation disrupt my business operations? expand_more
We design our installation process to minimize disruption to your daily operations. Most commercial installations are completed within 2-4 weeks depending on system size. Our team works around your business hours and can schedule critical work during off-peak times or weekends.
What financing options are available for businesses? expand_more
SolarForce offers flexible financing options: CAPEX (own the system, maximum savings and tax benefits), OPEX/PPA (zero upfront investment, pay discounted tariff for power generated), and solar loans with attractive interest rates through our banking partners. Choose the model that fits your financial goals.
What is net metering and how does it work? expand_more
Net metering allows you to export excess solar power back to the grid for credits. During peak sun hours when your system generates more than you consume, the excess is fed to the grid. These credits offset your electricity costs during non-solar hours, maximizing your savings.
What warranty and maintenance support do you provide? expand_more
We provide 25-year performance warranty on panels and 5-10 year warranty on inverters. Our comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) include regular cleaning, system health checks, real-time monitoring, and prompt issue resolution to ensure optimal performance throughout the system's life.
Can solar work for my factory or warehouse with high power consumption? expand_more
Absolutely! Factories and warehouses are ideal candidates for solar due to their large roof areas and high energy consumption. We design high-capacity systems (50kW to several MW) tailored to industrial load profiles. The larger your consumption, the faster your payback period and greater your savings.

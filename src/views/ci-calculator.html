Calculation Assumptions & Methodology

wb_sunny

Solar Generation

  • Irradiance: 4.0-6.0 kWh/m²/day (region-specific)
  • Performance Ratio: 78% (system efficiency)
  • Degradation: 2% Year 1, 0.5%/year thereafter
  • System Life: 25 years
payments

Financial Parameters

  • EPC Cost: ₹48-52K/kW (installation type)
  • O&M Cost: 1% of CAPEX/year
  • Tariff Escalation: 4%/year
  • Discount Rate: 10% (for NPV)
space_dashboard

Space Requirements

  • Rooftop: ~100 sq.ft per kW
  • Ground-mounted: ~120 sq.ft per kW
  • Net Metering Limit: 500 kW (typical)
  • Panel Capacity: 540-550W modules

function Key Formulas Used

System Capacity:
kW = Annual_kWh / (Peak_Sun_Hours × 365 × PR)
Annual Generation:
kWh = kW × Peak_Sun_Hours × 365 × PR × Degradation
NPV:
NPV = -Investment + Σ(Cashflow_t / (1+r)^t)
LCOE:
LCOE = Total_Discounted_Cost / Total_Discounted_Energy
